Frequently Asked Questions

Are these templates legally compliant in Nigeria?
Yes, all templates are designed to be compliant with Nigerian tenancy and property laws. However, we recommend consulting with a legal professional for specific legal advice related to your particular situation.
Do I need Microsoft Word to use these templates?
While templates are provided in Microsoft Word (.docx) format for easy editing, you can also use free alternatives like Google Docs, LibreOffice, or Apple Pages to open and edit these files.
Can I customize these templates for my specific needs?
Absolutely! These templates are fully editable. You can add, remove, or modify any sections to suit your specific property management requirements. All text and formatting can be customized.
Are there any hidden costs or subscriptions?
No, all templates on this page are completely free to download and use. There are no hidden costs, subscriptions, or usage limitations. We provide these resources to help the Nigerian property community.
