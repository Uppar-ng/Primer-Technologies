Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services and processes.

We can typically list your property within 48 hours of receiving all necessary documentation and completing our professional photography session. Our expedited service can list properties within 24 hours for urgent situations.

We currently serve 25 cities across multiple states including California, New York, Texas, Florida, and Illinois. Our services are expanding, so please contact us to check if we cover your specific area.

We offer flexible maintenance plans starting from basic monthly check-ups to comprehensive annual packages. All maintenance is performed by certified professionals, and we provide detailed reports after each service visit.

Yes, all our moving and logistics team members are fully insured, background-checked, and certified. We provide valuation coverage for your belongings and have a perfect safety record.

We offer a wide range of handyman services including minor repairs, installations, assembly, painting, drywall, plumbing fixes, electrical work (non-licensed), furniture assembly, and general home improvements.

You can schedule viewings directly through our website on any property page, via our mobile app, or by calling our office. We offer flexible scheduling including evenings and weekends.

Our fees vary by service and are transparently listed on our services page. Property listing typically ranges from 1-3% depending on the service package. Maintenance and handyman services are billed hourly or through monthly plans.
