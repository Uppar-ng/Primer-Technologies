Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on Zaure?
Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner. Fill in your name, phone number, and select your state. Once submitted, you'll have full access to buy, sell, and save favorites.
Is Zaure really free to use?
Yes! Zaure is completely free. There are no listing fees, no commission charges, and no hidden costs. We believe in connecting buyers and sellers directly.
How do I list an item for sale?
Click the "Post a listing" button or the "+" icon in the bottom navigation. Fill in your product details, upload images, and submit. Your listing will appear immediately on the platform.
How do I contact a seller?
Each product listing has a "Contact Seller" button with the seller's phone number. You can call or WhatsApp them directly. No middlemen involved.
How do I save my favorite items?
Click the heart icon on any product card or the "Save" button on the product detail page. Your favorites will appear in the Favorites section.
Is my personal information safe?
Absolutely. We take privacy and security seriously. Your data is stored securely and never shared with third parties. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.
