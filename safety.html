Verify Before You Trust

Always check seller/buyer verification status. Verified users have completed identity checks.

  • Look for the verified badge
  • Check user ratings and reviews
  • Ask for additional proof if needed

Meet in Public Places

Always arrange to meet in a public, well-lit location when completing a transaction.

  • Choose busy shopping areas
  • Bring a friend if possible
  • Avoid late-night meetings

Inspect Before You Pay

Always inspect the item thoroughly before making any payment.

  • Test electronics before buying
  • Check for damages or defects
  • Verify authenticity of branded items

Safe Payment Methods

Use secure payment methods and avoid sending money upfront without seeing the item.

  • Pay only after inspection
  • Use bank transfers with confirmation
  • Avoid Western Union or gift cards

Direct Communication

Contact sellers directly through the platform's provided channels.

  • Use the contact button on listings
  • Save all communication records
  • Trust your instincts

Report Suspicious Activity

If something feels wrong, report it immediately. We take all reports seriously.

  • Use the "Report" button on listings
  • Contact support directly
  • Block suspicious users
