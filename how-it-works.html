How Zaure Works

Four simple steps to find or list a home, without the middleman.

1

Owner Lists Property

Owners post their property for free with photos, price, and description. No agent required — you control everything.

2

Verify Identity

Owners can verify their identity via NIN or BVN to earn a trusted "Verified" badge, giving seekers confidence.

3

Connect Directly

Seekers browse listings, see the owner's phone number, and call or WhatsApp them directly. No agent screening calls.

4

Move In

Agree on terms, sign your agreement, and get the keys. The transaction is entirely between you and the owner.
