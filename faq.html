Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using Zaure safely and effectively.

What is Zaure?

Zaure is a free classifieds platform that connects property owners directly with seekers. We don't employ agents, charge commissions, or insert ourselves into transactions. Think of us as a digital entrance hall (that's what "zaure" means in Hausa) — we provide the space for you to meet and make your own deal.

Is Zaure really free?

Yes. Listing a property is completely free. Browsing and searching are free. Contacting an owner is free. We do not charge any fees or commissions on transactions. In the future we may introduce optional premium features (like boosting a listing), but we will never charge per transaction.

How do I list my property?

Go to the List Property page, fill in the details (title, location, price, photos, etc.), and submit. Your listing will be reviewed and published within a few hours. You can also request identity verification to earn a "Verified" badge, which makes seekers more likely to contact you.

What does the "Verified" badge mean?

A Verified badge means the owner has completed identity verification (NIN or BVN) through our third-party KYC provider. It confirms that the person's government ID is real and matches their name. It does NOT mean we have inspected the property or vouch for the person's character. Always do your own due diligence.

How do I stay safe when dealing with an owner or seeker?

We recommend the following:

  • Always view the property in person before paying any money.
  • Never send money to someone you haven't met.
  • Ask to see proof of ownership (C-of-O, deed, etc.) before making a payment.
  • Bring someone with you to viewings.
  • Trust your instincts — if something feels off, walk away and report the listing.
Does Zaure handle payments?

No. All financial transactions happen directly between the owner and the seeker. We do not collect rent, sale proceeds, or deposits. We recommend using traceable payment methods and documenting everything.

How do I report a fake listing or suspicious behaviour?

Please email us at get.zaure@proton.me with the property link and details of what happened. We review reports within 12 hours and will remove fraudulent listings immediately. If you feel threatened or have been scammed, please also contact the local authorities.

How do I edit or remove my listing?

Currently, contact us at get.zaure@proton.me with your property ID and the changes you need. We're building a self-service dashboard that will be available soon.

Which cities does Zaure cover?

Zaure is focused on Northern Nigeria, with properties currently in Kaduna, Kano, Zaria, Abuja, Sokoto, Maiduguri, Jos, and more. We are expanding steadily. If your city isn't listed yet, you can still post a property — we'll make it visible.

How can I contact Zaure?

Visit our Contact page, or email us at get.zaure@proton.me. We respond within 24 hours.
