## Overview

Zaure Technologies operates a free, direct-connection platform for property owners and seekers. We are not agents. We do not take a cut of your rent or sale. We simply provide a trusted digital space where you list your property and serious tenants or buyers contact you directly.

### Primary Users
- **Property Owners** — List homes for sale or rent, get verified, receive direct calls
- **Home Seekers** — Browse verified listings, contact owners directly, no agent fees

### Regions Served
Kaduna · Kano · Zaria · Abuja · Sokoto · Maiduguri · Jos · Katsina · Bauchi · Gombe

## Features

### 🏠 Core Platform
- **Direct Owner-to-Seeker** — No agents, no commission fees
- **Verified Badge System** — One-time ₦500 NIN/BVN verification for owners
- **Property Listings** — Buy, rent, land, and commercial properties
- **Location-Based Discovery** — GPS/IP detection shows properties near you
- **City Browsing** — Browse by city across Northern Nigeria

### 📱 User Experience
- **TikTok-Style "For You" Feed** — Vertical swipe to discover properties
- **Video & Image Carousel** — Video tours or swipeable image galleries
- **Dark/Light Mode** — Theme toggle with localStorage persistence
- **Mobile-Responsive Design** — Optimized for all devices
- **WhatsApp Integration** — Direct sharing and contact via WhatsApp

### 🔒 Trust & Safety
- **Owner Verification** — NIN/BVN validation for verified badges
- **Direct Communication** — Call or WhatsApp owners directly from listings
- **Safety Guidelines** — Built-in safety tips for seekers
