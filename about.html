About Zaure

We are building a trusted space where property owners and seekers meet directly — without agents, hidden fees, or middlemen.

Our Story

Zaure was born out of a simple frustration: finding a house to rent or buy in Northern Nigeria meant dealing with agents who often lacked transparency, charged arbitrary fees, and controlled access to listings.

We saw that property owners wanted to connect directly with tenants and buyers, but lacked a trusted platform to do so. Seekers were tired of paying "viewing fees" before even seeing a home.

Named after the traditional Hausa entrance hall — the zaure — our platform is that first room where guests are welcomed and genuine connections begin. We provide the space; the transaction is yours.

Today, Zaure is growing across Kano, Kaduna, Zaria, Abuja, and beyond — built by a team passionate about making property search honest, simple, and human.
