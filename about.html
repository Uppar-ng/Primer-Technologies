About Primer Technologies

Building lasting relationships through complete property solutions

Our Story

Founded in 2026, Primer Technologies began with a simple mission: to simplify the property experience for everyone. We saw that buying, selling, and maintaining properties was fragmented, stressful, and inefficient.

Our founders, Alamin Ahmed and Najib Isah Ukissa, combined their expertise in real estate and technology to create a platform that addresses every aspect of property ownership and management in one place.

What started as a small local service has grown into a comprehensive property solutions company serving hundreds of clients across multiple states. We're proud to have helped over 50 families find their dream homes and manage their properties seamlessly.
