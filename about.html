About Prima

Complete property solutions for modern living in Nigeria.

Our Story

Prima is a real estate technology platform headquartered in Northern Nigeria, built to simplify the way people buy, rent, and maintain properties. Launched in 2026, we saw a gap in the market: property transactions and home services were fragmented, stressful, and lacked transparency.

Our founders blended deep real‑estate experience with modern technology and a customer‑first mindset to create a complete ecosystem. From property search and valuation to maintenance, moving, and handyman services – everything is connected in one trusted platform

What began as a small local service has rapidly grown into one of Northern Nigeria’s most reliable property technology partners, serving hundreds of clients across Kaduna, Kano, Abuja, and beyond. We are proud to have helped over 500 families find their dream homes and manage their properties seamlessly.

At Prima, we believe that technology should make property ownership simpler, not harder. Our commitment to transparency, integrity, and innovation drives every decision we make – because your home deserves a smarter solution.
