Our Story

Zaure was born from a clear market failure: in Northern Nigeria, finding a home meant navigating a fragmented system of intermediaries who controlled access, distorted pricing, and offered little accountability.

Property owners lacked a direct channel to reach serious tenants and buyers. Seekers were routinely asked to pay "viewing fees" before seeing a single room.

We built Zaure to close that gap. Named after the traditional Hausa entrance hall—the zaure—our platform serves as the trusted threshold where verified owners and genuine seekers meet.

Behind Zaure is Alamin Ahmed, a passionate advocate for digital inclusion in Northern Nigeria. As Founder & CEO, Alamin brings deep local market knowledge and a commitment to leveraging technology for social impact.
